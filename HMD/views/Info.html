Keys
  • Blue: Excess - Stock on hand is above Max
  • Green : Normal - Stock on hand is between Min and Max
  • Orange: BelowMin - Stock on hand is below Min and above 0
  • Red : StockedOut - Stock on hand is 0
Definition
  • Min: Minimum stock that the Hub should have - 3 months of stock
  • Max: Maximum stock that the Hub should have - 6 months of stock
  • AMC: Average Monthly Consumption = ([TotalIssue]/([FiscalYeardays-DOS])) * 30
  • SOH: Stock on Hand
  • MOS = SOH/AMC
